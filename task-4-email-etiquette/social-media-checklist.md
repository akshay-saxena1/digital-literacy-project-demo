# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist  

---

## ✅ Do's

- **Think before you post**  
  Take a moment to consider how your post might be perceived by others.

- **Maintain privacy settings**  
  Regularly check and update your account privacy to control who sees your content.

- **Be respectful and kind**  
  Treat others online the same way you would in real life.

- **Share accurate information**  
  Verify facts before posting or sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.

- **Build a positive digital presence**  
  Post content that reflects your personality, achievements, and values in a good way.

---

## ❌ Don'ts

- **Don't share personal or sensitive information**  
  Avoid posting your address, phone number, passwords, or private details.

- **Don't engage in online arguments or hate speech**  
  Stay away from toxic discussions that can harm your reputation.

- **Don't post inappropriate content**  
  Avoid content that could be offensive, unprofessional, or damaging in the future.

- **Don't overshare your daily life**  
  Too much information can affect your privacy and safety.

- **Don't ignore your digital footprint**  
  Remember that what you post online can stay there permanently.

---

## 📌 Final Tip

Your social media is a reflection of you — use it wisely and responsibly!
